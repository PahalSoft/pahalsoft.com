Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

MyMoneyWiz (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the MyMoneyWiz mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MyMoneyWiz is designed to work primarily offline. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal financial data on our servers.

  • Financial records you enter are stored locally on your device
  • We do not access your bank accounts
  • We do not read your messages or emails

2. Data Storage

All financial data (transactions, assets, reports) is stored locally on your device unless you choose to back it up using your own cloud or device backup settings.

3. Permissions

MyMoneyWiz may request basic permissions only when required for app functionality (such as storage access for backups). These permissions are never used to collect personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

MyMoneyWiz does not share your financial data with third parties. If analytics or crash reporting tools are used, they only collect anonymous technical information to improve app stability.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for protecting access to your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

MyMoneyWiz is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
